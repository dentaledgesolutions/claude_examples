## Pricing Notes

- **Bundle discount:** Combining 3 or more à la carte services qualifies for a 25% discount on the combined monthly total.
- **Setup fees** cover real onboarding work: campaign research, workflow builds, AI training, A2P compliance registration, and staff training.
- **SEO add-ons** (Local SEO Boost and SEO/AEO/GEO Program) are stackable with any package tier.
- **Market context:** Full-service dental marketing agencies nationally charge $2,100–$10,000/month. Patient communication software (Weave, RevenueWell, Adit) runs $300–$800/month for tools the practice must manage themselves.
- **Geography:** All services are delivered exclusively for SE Florida independent dental practices (Miami-Dade, Broward, Palm Beach).
