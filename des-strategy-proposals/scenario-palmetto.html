The Challenge

Great Care. Zero Visibility. A City That Speaks Spanish.

Palmetto Dental Studio faces the steepest climb of any practice in this portfolio — not because of poor quality, but because of market dynamics. Hialeah is one of the most concentrated dental markets in South Florida, and the leading practices have built massive review moats over years. Starting at 68 reviews with no Spanish digital presence is a compounded disadvantage.

  • 📍
    Completely absent from Google Maps for primary market keyword "Dentist Hialeah FL" returns 10 competitors — none of them is Palmetto. The top result has 1,105 reviews; even the #10 practice has more reviews than Palmetto.
  • ⭐
    68 reviews — the lowest starting point in a high-review market Hialeah's top-10 Map Pack averages 470 reviews per practice. At 68, Palmetto signals "new" to Google's algorithm regardless of actual quality — and new practices get ranked last.
  • 🇪🇸
    No Spanish-language presence in a market that's 85% Hispanic Hialeah is one of the most Spanish-speaking cities in the country. An English-only website and GBP is a trust gap that costs appointments — patients choose practices that speak to them in their language.
