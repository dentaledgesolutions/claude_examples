09 — Common Questions

Questions we hear — and the honest answers.

We'd rather answer the hard questions in this proposal than leave them for after you sign.

We've tried marketing agencies before and wasted money. Why is this different?
Most agencies deliver activity — ads, posts, a website — and disappear. We deliver a system that connects every channel to your CRM, tracks every lead through to a booked appointment, and shows you the ROI in real numbers. You don't pay us to run ads. You pay us to produce booked patients — and our reporting makes that visible every month.
We already use [Weave / RevenueWell / Adit]. Why would we switch?
Those are good tools that require your team to manage them. We take that burden completely off your plate and add capabilities those platforms don't have — high-value case funnels, AI voice agents, HIPAA-compliant AI review responses, multilingual communications, and ads management — all integrated in one system. Many of our clients replaced 3–4 separate subscriptions with DES and reduced their total monthly software spend while getting more capability.
What happens to our patient data? Is this HIPAA compliant?
Our system runs on GoHighLevel, configured under a Business Associate Agreement at the agency level. All patient communications are built with HIPAA guardrails — no clinical details in SMS, no PHI in email subject lines, and AI review responses are configured to never reference treatment or appointment-specific information. We brief your team on data handling obligations and maintain those standards across every workflow we build.
We use Dentrix / Eaglesoft. Will your system integrate with it?
GoHighLevel does not natively write back to dental practice management software (PMS). For appointments booked through our system — from ads, the website, the AI chatbot, or the voice agent — those are managed in our platform. Your PMS remains your clinical record system. Many practices run both in parallel. Where a bridge is needed, we identify a Zapier or manual workflow solution to keep everything in sync without disruption to your existing clinical operations.
How long until we see results?
The fastest ROI services go live in 24–48 hours — missed call text-back and appointment reminders typically produce measurable results within the first week. Review growth is visible within 30 days. Ad campaigns typically produce their first booked patients within 2 weeks of launch. Most clients see a clear, positive return on their DES investment before the first month is over.
What if we want to cancel?
Our packages are month-to-month after the initial setup period. We don't lock practices into long-term contracts because we'd rather earn your business every month than trap you in it. Setup fees cover the genuine onboarding work — website builds, workflow configuration, AI training, campaign research, A2P compliance registration, and staff training. That work has real labor cost regardless of how long you stay.
