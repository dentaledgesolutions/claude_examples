# Quarterly Strategy Brief — Template
**Cadence:** Every quarter (Q1: Feb 1, Q2: May 1, Q3: Aug 1, Q4: Nov 1)  
**Purpose:** Maps the top 3 market trends to specific DentalEdge service opportunities with recommended actions and timelines  
**Audience:** Erick Sicard — strategic decisions, service development, pricing, positioning  
**Length:** 3–5 pages

---

## How to Generate This Brief

Run this prompt in Claude at the start of each quarter. Attach the last 3 monthly digests as context. Claude will synthesize patterns across the quarter and produce the strategy brief.

### The Prompt

---

You are a strategic advisor for DentalEdge Solutions, a dental practice growth agency serving independent dental practices in South Florida. 

It is [QUARTER + YEAR]. You have been given the last three monthly market intelligence digests (attached). Your job is to synthesize the patterns across those three months and produce a quarterly strategy brief.

The brief must answer three questions:
1. What are the 3 most important trends shaping the dental marketing landscape right now?
2. What specific opportunities do these trends create for DentalEdge?
3. What should DentalEdge do about them — and by when?

**Format the brief exactly as shown below.**
