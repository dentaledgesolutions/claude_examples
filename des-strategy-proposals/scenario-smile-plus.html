The Challenge

A 4.8-Star Practice That No One Could Find

Smile Plus had everything a growing practice needs — clinical skills, loyal patients, and a sterling reputation. What they lacked was online visibility. Despite 286 five-star reviews, they didn't appear in the top 10 Google Maps results for "dentist Homestead FL."

  • 📍
    Invisible on Google Maps for every primary search term Searching "dentist Homestead FL" returns 10 competitors. Smile Plus is not among them — despite having more reviews than 6 of those 10 practices.
  • ⭐
    Review velocity stalled — no active collection system 286 reviews accumulated over years, but with no automated follow-up, new reviews trickle in one or two a month while competitors aggressively grow their counts.
  • 🇪🇸
    No Spanish-language digital presence in a bilingual market Over 70% of Homestead residents are Spanish-speaking. Smile Plus's website and Google Business Profile are English-only — a critical gap in a market where language signals trust.
